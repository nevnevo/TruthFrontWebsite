Truth Front AI

TruthFront AI is designed to give the user two primary tools.

The first tool is a debate-helper, which users can utilize when engaging in online debates. It provides active Hasbara users with an effective way to handle anti-Israeli claims, respond properly, and defend Israel confidently. It also serves as a helping hand when feeling stuck during an argument.

The second tool offers an educational experience: giving anti-Israelis a chance to test and rethink their claims. Through this process, they can learn more about our beloved nation and hopefully change their approach and thinking.

TruthFront continues to grow in the Hasbara realm, and we are committed to building the best tools possible and upgrading them continuously!
